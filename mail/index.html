About us

To engage in the business of healthcare solutions and manufacturing, marketing, conducting clinical and preclinical research in the field of pharmaceuticals and allied activities.

Who We Are

Dbotica mobile application helps users to get medical prescriptions delivered to their doorstep. Users can track their medical history and can consult/refer to new doctors. It is a new age technology that is providing a comprehensive and economical healthcare solution to users. We are currently operational in Bangalore and Delhi NCR.
