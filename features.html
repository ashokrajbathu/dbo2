3 Simple Steps to Get Your Prescriptions Online

1
Get a Prescription

Visit a Dbotica partnered Doctor/Clinic and get your digital prescription recorded by registering your phone number. You'll recieve a text message about new prescription

2
Download Dbotica App and Register

Click on the link in text message and download the app. Once you downloaded the app, register using your same phone number and verify it with one time password.

3
Get Online Reminders

Once logged in, you can access your digital prescription online anytime, anywhere and using Dbotica's MIRS system you can #SkipNoPills.
